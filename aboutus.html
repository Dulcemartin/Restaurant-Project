About us

It all started with the dream of opening up a restaurant that would offer real authentic Mexican food. But it could not be the same concept as other restaurants, we needed to offer a dish that would distinguish us from the rest. Thats where the Idea of selling freshly flame broiled Sinaloa-style chicken was born. We opened the doors to our first location back in 2017 in the city of Ogden and a year after we opened our second location in Taylorsville.

So what makes us different? Our love for Mexican cuisine and wanting to share this love with our communities.
